Production

P:01.

motorcycle disassembly

the motorcycle is dismantled to be able to do the work safely to avoid damage to the motorcycle and do a good job both electrically and in suspension and combustion engine.

P:02.

motorcycle paint

after the dismantling of the motorcycle, the motorcycle is painted so that it looks better and does not stay in the position of dismantling, so it will have a better aesthetic and the parts of the motorcycle can be seen better.

P:03.

electric motor

the electric motor is mounted and the position is adjusted including the controller and the energy generator, to have a greater accommodation and take advantage of all the available space

P:04.

preparation quote

all the preparations for the dismantling and painting of the motorcycle are quoted in addition to the materials for the motorcycle and the electric motor so that more components can be bought in the future and we make a world with less pollution and more cost savings.

From gasoline to electric

conversion of gasoline motorcycles to electric motorcycles to save money and have a better environment.

energy self-generator

the energy self-generator is a device that generates energy from the movement of the motorcycle, this energy is stored in a battery and is used to move the motorcycle.
